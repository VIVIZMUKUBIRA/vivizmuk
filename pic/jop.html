About Us

Our story and mission

Viviz Mubira Junior School

Established in 2005, Viviz Mubira Junior School has been at the forefront of providing quality education to children in our community. We believe in nurturing each child's unique talents and abilities.

Our mission is to provide a safe, stimulating learning environment that promotes academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning skills.
